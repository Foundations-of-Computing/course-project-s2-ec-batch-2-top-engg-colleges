Government Engineering College (GEC), Thrissur

About GEC Thrissur

Government Engineering College, Thrissur (GEC Thrissur) is one of the premier engineering institutions in Kerala, India. It was established in 1957.

Campuses:

GEC Thrissur has a single main campus located in Thrissur, Kerala.

Academic Programs:

GEC Thrissur offers a wide range of undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs in fields such as engineering, technology, and management. Some popular courses include BTech, MTech, MBA, and PhD.

Research and Innovation:

The college is known for its strong emphasis on research and innovation, particularly in areas like civil engineering, mechanical engineering, and electrical engineering. It has collaborations with various industries and research institutions.

Admissions:

Admissions to many programs are based on the KEAM (Kerala Engineering Architecture Medical) entrance exam. Some courses also consider national-level exams like GATE.

Facilities:

  • Well-equipped laboratories and libraries.
  • Hostel accommodations for students.
  • Sports and recreational facilities.

Reputation:

GEC Thrissur is consistently ranked among the top engineering colleges in Kerala. It is affiliated with APJ Abdul Kalam Technological University and accredited by NBA.

Location Advantage:

Thrissur is a cultural capital of Kerala, offering a vibrant cultural and professional environment. The city is well-connected by air, rail, and road, making it accessible for students from across India and abroad.
