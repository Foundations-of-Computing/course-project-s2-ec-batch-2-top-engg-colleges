Indian Institute of Space Science and Technology (IIST), Thiruvananthapuram

About IIST

Indian Institute of Space Science and Technology (IIST) is a government-aided institute and deemed university for the study and research of space science, located in Thiruvananthapuram, Kerala, India. It was founded in 2007.

Campuses:

IIST has a single campus located in Valiamala, Thiruvananthapuram.

Academic Programs:

IIST offers undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs in space science and technology. Some popular courses include BTech in Aerospace Engineering, Avionics, and Dual Degree (BTech + MS/MTech).

Research and Innovation:

The institute is known for its strong emphasis on research in space science and technology. It has collaborations with ISRO and other national and international space agencies.

Admissions:

Admissions to undergraduate programs are based on the JEE Advanced exam. Postgraduate admissions consider GATE scores and other national-level exams.

Facilities:

  • State-of-the-art laboratories and research centers.
  • Hostel accommodations for students.
  • Sports and recreational facilities.

Reputation:

IIST is highly regarded for its specialized education and research in space science and technology. It is recognized as a premier institute in India.

Location Advantage:

Thiruvananthapuram is the capital city of Kerala, known for its rich cultural heritage and educational institutions. The city is well-connected by air, rail, and road, making it accessible for students from across India and abroad.
