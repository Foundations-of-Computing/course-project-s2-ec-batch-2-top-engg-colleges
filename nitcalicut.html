National Institute of Technology (NIT), Calicut

About NIT Calicut

National Institute of Technology Calicut (NIT Calicut) is one of the premier technical institutions in India. It is located in Kattangal, Kerala, and is known for its academic excellence, research contributions, and vibrant campus life

1. Overview

Established:

1961 (as Calicut Regional Engineering College, became NIT Calicut in 2002).

Type:

Public technical university.

Location:

Kattangal, Kozhikode District, Kerala, India.

Campus:,

Spread over 120 hectares, the campus is known for its scenic beauty and state-of-the-art infrastructure.

Accreditation:

Accredited by NAAC with an 'A' grade and recognized by UGC.

Institute of National Importance:

Designated as an Institute of National Importance under the NIT Act, 2007.

2. Academics

Departments:

NIT Calicut offers undergraduate (UG), postgraduate (PG), and doctoral (Ph.D.) programs in various disciplines.

Some of the key departments include:

  • Computer Science and Engineering (CSE)
  • Electrical Engineering (EE)
  • Mechanical Engineering (ME)
  • Civil Engineering (CE)
  • Electronics and Communication Engineering (ECE)
  • Chemical Engineering (ChE)
  • Architecture and Planning (B.Arch)
  • Mathematics, Physics, Chemistry, and Humanities.

Programs Offered:

  • Undergraduate: B.Tech, B.Arch.
  • Postgraduate: M.Tech, M.Sc, MBA, MCA.
  • Doctoral: Ph.D. in various disciplines.

Admissions:

  • UG: Through JEE Main (Joint Entrance Examination).
  • PG: Through GATE (for M.Tech) and CAT/CMAT (for MBA).
  • Ph.D.: Based on written tests and interviews.

4. Campus Life

Hostels:

NIT Calicut has separate hostels for boys and girls with modern amenities.

Clubs and Societies:

The campus has numerous student-run clubs and societies, including:
  • Technical clubs (e.g., Robotics Club, Coding Club).
  • Cultural clubs (e.g., Music Club, Dance Club).
  • Sports clubs (e.g., Cricket, Football, Badminton).

Festivals:

  • Tathva: The annual technical festival.
  • Ragam: The annual cultural festival.

Sports Facilities:

The campus has facilities for cricket, football, basketball, tennis, and more.
